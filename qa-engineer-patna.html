QA FAQs

Common questions about software testing services in Patna.

What does a QA Engineer do?

A QA (Quality Assurance) Engineer ensures that software products meet specific requirements and are bug-free. They perform manual and automated testing to identify issues before the product reaches the users.

Why is software testing important?

Software testing prevents costly post-launch bug fixes, protects your brand reputation, and ensures a seamless user experience, which is critical for business success.

Do you provide remote QA services?

What testing tools do you use?
